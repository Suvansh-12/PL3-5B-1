A more personal experience

Remote working has always been around but has really come to the fore in the last 18 months with lockdowns and restrictions making many of us work from home and having to adjust our set-ups accordingly, although it may also be time to look at refreshing your home office setup.

Some of us have taken the opportunity to become digital nomads and work from different places in the world.

This flexibility provides the necessary mobility and adaptability in our workspaces and equipment to make this remote work as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

The Home Office by Worky

The Home Office by Worky enables you to turn any space into a portable, personal workstation that you can pack away into a convenient carry case at the end of the day.

The workstation has compartments for all your cables, folders, devices, and stationery needs. It has built-in power and charging including 2 standard outlets, 1x USB-A, and 1x USB-C port.
